Menstraution: the monthly shedding of the lining of a person's uterus (also known as the womb). This happens during a person's cycle when the body prepares for pregnancy but there is none.


In the United States about 90% of people who menstrate state they experience various symptoms, 32%-40% report to have pain so intense they can't continue with their normal schedules and 14% have painful, irregular, or heavy periods in childbearing years.

Sexually Transmitted Diseases: Sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), or sexually transmitted infections (STIs), are infections that are passed from one person to another through sexual contact. The contact is commonly vaginal, oral, and anal sex.
